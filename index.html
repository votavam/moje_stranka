Crispy Fried Schnitzel

A classic crispy schnitzel recipe – golden on the outside and juicy on the inside.

Preparation time:
  • Total: 30 minutes
  • Preparation: 15 minutes
  • Cooking: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 pork or chicken cutlets
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 eggs
  • Flour
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Pound the meat until thin.
  2. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Coat in flour, egg, and breadcrumbs.
  4. Fry 3–4 minutes per side until golden.

Nutrition (per serving)

Calories: 450 kcal

Protein: 35 g

Fat: 25 g
